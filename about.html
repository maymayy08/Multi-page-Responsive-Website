About Me

"Art is not just a creation; it's the heartbeat of passion, a language that speaks where words fall short."

Greetings! I'm May and I first learned how to make origami from my mum when I was 13, who have introduced me to the world of art. I remember when she first learn how to make orgami, I would always sitting beside her and watching her in awe as she effortlessly transformed a piece a paper into a crane or a rose with just a few precise folds.

To save time, I would always help her to fold pieces of paper to speed up things faster. She taught me that making origami wasn't just about following the steps; but it was about embracing the process and paying attention to details. Therefore, I myself have decided to take on challenge what I have learned from her and take it from there.

Few months later, I decided to gift my creations to close familys and friends and they was amazed how everything is just out of paper!
